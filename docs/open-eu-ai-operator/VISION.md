# Open EU AI Operator — Vision

## Purpose

Open EU AI Operator is a governance and compliance framework for entrepreneurs and small teams deploying AI systems in the European Union and EU-adjacent markets (including the Caribbean Dutch territories of Curaçao, Aruba, and Sint Maarten).

It provides practical, opinionated templates for the EU AI Act, GDPR, and ethical AI deployment — designed for operators without dedicated legal or compliance teams.

## Problem

Most EU AI Act compliance resources are written for large enterprises with legal departments. Small operators — freelancers, agencies, SaaS founders, and consultants — face the same obligations but with none of the infrastructure.

The gap: obligations are real, tooling is absent, guidance is inaccessible.

## Mission

Make EU-compliant AI deployment achievable for a solo operator or a team of five — without hiring a compliance consultant.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Practical over theoretical.** Every document must be actionable. No guidance that cannot be turned into a checklist or a template.
2. **Transparent by default.** AI systems built with this framework disclose their nature, limitations, and data usage to end users.
3. **Human review is non-negotiable.** For any AI output that affects a person's rights, safety, or finances, a human reviews before delivery.
4. **Minimal data, maximum trust.** Collect only what is needed. Retain only what is legally required. Delete the rest.
5. **Caribbean and global context.** The framework acknowledges that Curaçao and the broader Caribbean operate in a unique legal intersection of Dutch, EU, and local law.

## Scope

The framework covers:

- AI system classification (risk tiers under the EU AI Act)
- GDPR data mapping and processing records
- Transparency obligations (user-facing disclosures)
- Incident and error logging for AI outputs
- Contributor onboarding for open-source AI projects
- Sector-specific starter packs (hospitality, real estate, marketing)

## Out of Scope

- Legal advice (consult a qualified attorney for your jurisdiction)
- Technical AI model development
- Payments, invoicing, or financial compliance

## Target Users

| User | Use case |
|---|---|
| Solo AI consultant | Deploy compliant AI automations for SME clients |
| SaaS founder | Ship EU-market products with built-in compliance |
| Agency owner | Standardise AI delivery across client projects |
| Caribbean entrepreneur | Navigate EU regulatory overlap in Dutch territories |
